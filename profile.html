COMPANY PROFILE

Established in the year 2007, we IESAAN ENERGY, engaged in manufacturing and exporting a wide range of Industrial Noise Control Products such as Acoustic Enclosures, Silencers etc. We manufacture these products using superior quality raw material that is procured from the reliable vendors of the market. Being highly durable and superlative in quality, these products are extensively used in Industrial & commercial sector. We scrutinize our range on every step of production and also conduct third party inspection from some of the renowned agencies such as LLOYDS, BVIS, DNV and TUV. Further, to suit the specific requirements of our client's, we also offer customization facilities.
